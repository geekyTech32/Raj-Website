About Me

My 29-year culinary journey has seen me evolve through diverse roles, from fine dining to high-volume hotel kitchens. As Executive Chef at Mirage al Bab Resort and Tower, I've crafted innovative menus and led talented teams, ensuring quality and creativity in every dish. My global experiences, spanning the UK, Russia, and more, have enriched my culinary perspective. With strong organizational skills, I've managed budgets and maintained efficiency. My ability to lead under pressure and create exceptional dining experiences makes me a strong candidate for the Executive Chef position.

Achivement

Throughout my extensive culinary career, I've successfully opened multiple restaurants from scratch, overseeing everything from kitchen setup to menu creation. Notably, I led the Black Horse Pub in Hounslow to become the best in the airport area with dishes like fish and chips, chicken tikka, and kebabs. In Russia, as a Senior Tandoor Chef, I introduced smoky flavors, making our restaurant a hit. Recently, at Mirage al Bahr Resort and Hotel Fujairah, I oversaw the culinary department, opening three restaurants and two cafes, ensuring exceptional dining experiences for guests worldwide.
